Wellwatch's mission is to empower people to understand and improve their health

To accomplish our mission,people need complete access and control over their health information,and thhey need to be able to understand it simply to make informed decisions. We're building a solution that is bridgind the information gap.

Our goal is to build an enduring website that measurably improves both our own health and the health of our fellow humans worldwide. This is reflected in our website model - we reach people who are trying to keep you healthy so that our incentives are directly aligned with your health goals.
